Purpose

Fortaleza city is located in the northeast of Brazil. It has a small geographical area, being formed by a flat relief, marked by the presence of coastal plains. The city is bathed by the Atlantic Ocean, and its coastline is approximately 34 kilometers long and divided into 15 beaches. We assist travelers, companies and associations that want to know more about the city. Join us.
