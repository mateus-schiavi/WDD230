Audience

People aged 18+ who are looking to travel around the world and become a member of this agency, also, including those who are businessman/businesswoman that are looking for a partnership in the travel commerce

Persona 1 and Scenario

Ichigo Kurosaki is a japanese citizen who has a degree in Photography. Two years ago, he opened a web store focused on photography of many places around the world. Willing to take some photos of Brazil, he accessed google, and found Fortaleza's Travel Agency, and now he can come to Brazil to take wonderful pictures and achieve his goal.

Persona 2 and Scenario

Vasco da Gama is 32 years old and has a degree in Business Management. Six years ago he opened a web store focused on digital marketing. Willing to expand his business, he discovered the Fortaleza's Travel Agency while talking with a friend. Now, he wants to join us to help the agency to achieve better conditions.

Scenario 3

People who wants to travel around Brazil and around the world, people who wants to become a member of the agency and people who wants to make new deals with us. Also, we assist those who wants to work with traveling.
