A brief history of our company

The Fortaleza Chamber of Commerce: Navigating the Challenges of 2022-2023 Founding and Inaugural Year (2022):
In the vibrant city of Fortaleza, Brazil, 2022 marked the birth of a new enterprise â€“ the Fortaleza Chamber of Commerce. The city was thriving with diverse businesses and a rapidly expanding economy. A group of visionary entrepreneurs, government officials, and community leaders came together to create a platform that would support local businesses, foster economic growth, and promote Fortaleza as a regional economic powerhouse. The Chamber officially opened its doors in a modern, purpose-built office in the city center.
Digital Transformation and Pandemic Response (2022 - 2023):
The Chamber quickly adapted to the challenges posed by the ongoing global pandemic. It launched a robust digital infrastructure to support remote work, online networking, and e-commerce initiatives for local businesses. These efforts not only sustained many enterprises but also facilitated growth in new areas like online retail and digital services. The Chamber became a hub for knowledge sharing and crisis management, helping businesses navigate the uncertain times.
Sustainable Business Practices (2022 - 2023):
Environmental concerns were high on the Chamber's agenda. In response to growing awareness of sustainability, the Chamber initiated programs to encourage eco-friendly business practices. It offered resources and incentives for companies to reduce their environmental footprint. Fortaleza became a leading city in Brazil for green business initiatives, attracting environmentally conscious entrepreneurs and investors.
Economic Recovery and Resilience (2022 - 2023):
The pandemic's economic impact was substantial, and Fortaleza faced its share of challenges. The Chamber of Commerce played a pivotal role in coordinating efforts to secure financial aid for struggling businesses, worked with local and regional governments to formulate recovery plans, and provided resources and support to businesses to rebuild and adapt to the new normal.
International Engagement and Trade (2022 - 2023):
Fortaleza's strategic location made it a gateway for international trade. The Chamber actively engaged with foreign trade partners, fostering international relationships that opened up new opportunities for local businesses. Trade missions and partnerships with foreign entities became common, contributing to the city's economic diversification.
Legacy (2023):
As 2023 drew to a close, the Fortaleza Chamber of Commerce had established itself as a vital institution in the city's business community. It had successfully navigated the challenging period of its inception and emerged as a beacon of support, growth, and innovation for local businesses. The Chamber's legacy was one of adaptability, sustainability, and resilience, ensuring that Fortaleza continued to prosper and thrive, even in the face of adversity. The years 2022-2023 had set the foundation for a bright and promising future for Fortaleza and its businesses.

The owner:
Mateus de Sousa Schiavi, the visionary owner of the Fortaleza Chamber of Commerce, is a dedicated and forward-thinking entrepreneur. Born and raised in Fortaleza, Mateus has a deep-rooted passion for the city and a strong commitment to its economic and social well-being.
With a background in business management and a keen understanding of local and international markets, Mateus founded the Chamber in 2022, driven by a desire to foster economic growth, innovation, and sustainability in Fortaleza.
A charismatic and influential leader, Mateus de Sousa Schiavi possesses a remarkable ability to bring together diverse groups of people, from entrepreneurs and business owners to government officials and community leaders.
His leadership has been instrumental in navigating the challenges of the pandemic, promoting sustainable business practices, and positioning Fortaleza as a thriving hub for both local and international trade.
Mateus's unwavering dedication to Fortaleza and his unwavering commitment to the Chamber's mission have made him a respected figure in both business and community circles.
Under his guidance, the Chamber has made significant strides in ensuring the city's prosperity, and his vision for Fortaleza's future remains a driving force behind the Chamber's success.
